Black Ops Series

On February 25, 1968, SAD operative Alex Mason is strapped to a chair in an interrogation room, he is asked multiple questions by his unseen interragators about the location of a numbers station. Mason then recalls several events, as an attempt to answer their questions. In 1961, Mason, Woods, and Bowman take part in Operation 40 to assassinate Fidel Castro in Cuba during the Bay of Pigs Invasion. Mason apparently succeeds and stays behind to protect the extraction plane from an incoming artilary strike, but they did not successfully assassinate Fidel for theyhave killed one of his doubles. He is then handed over to General Dragovich to be held captive at Vorkuta prison camp, Mason befriends inmate and former Red Army soldier, Viktor Reznov. Reznov recounts to Mason the identities of his enemies, Dragovich, Kravchenko, and Friedrich Steiner.
