Call of Duty

Call of Duty is one of the most popular first person shooters out there. It started out small when it came out in 2003 with its first release,Call of Duty.

First Major Release

2003-2006 was very uneventful for the Call of Duty franchise. But in 2004 everything changed. Infinity Ward came out with Call of Duty 4 Modern Warfare.

When CoD 4 hit the market, millions of people were playing. People have never seen a game that was set in the modern time period. And what made this game so unique is that it brought perks, killstreaks, and prestiges. This is why Call of Duty 4 is the most iconic Call of Duty or even the most iconic fps game out there.
